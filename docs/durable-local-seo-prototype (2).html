Local SEO Automation for Service Businesses

Durable has created 6M+ websites in 30 seconds using AI. However, user reviews indicate a critical gap: "Limited SEO capabilities."

Service business owners create Durable websites in 30 seconds but remain invisible in local search, leading to zero customers and high churn. They lack SEO knowledge and can't afford agencies ($500-2000/month).

Solution 1: Priority Action Queue

AI-powered action prioritization that shows users exactly what to do next and the measurable impact of each action.

Problem This Solves

Users face "SEO paralysis" - they don't know where to start, what matters most, or if their actions are working. Traditional SEO tools present 20+ items without prioritization, causing overwhelm and inaction.

Key Innovation

Instead of overwhelming users with a 20-item SEO checklist, AI identifies the top 3 actions with the highest impact-to-effort ratio. Each action shows:

  • • Exact rank improvement: "+3 positions" not "improves SEO"

  • • Secondary benefits: "+15% profile views" shows full value
  • • One-click execution: Button does everything automatically

Key Innovation

Citation building is the most tedious part of local SEO. It requires:

  • • Finding 50+ relevant directories (3 hours research)
  • • Creating accounts on each (4 hours, 5 min × 50)
  • • Filling out forms (8 hours, 10 min × 50)
  • • Verifying via email/phone (2 hours)
  • • Monitoring for changes (ongoing)
  • Total: 17+ hours + ongoing maintenance

With Durable Local: 1 click, 24 hours, done. This transforms citation building from "too hard, never do it" to "easy, everyone does it."
