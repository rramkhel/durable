About This Prototype

This interactive demo was created by Rachel Ramkhelawan as part of my application for the Product Manager role at Durable.

The prototype addresses a real user pain point identified in reviews: "Limited SEO capabilities". Local SEO is critical for service businesses but requires specialized knowledge most small business owners don't have.

Key Innovation: Instead of teaching users SEO, Durable Local automates the entire process. Users get actionable recommendations with clear impact metrics ("+3 ranks", "+40% traffic"), making SEO accessible without expertise.
